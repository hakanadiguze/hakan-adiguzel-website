About Me

Scaling agility across teams & organizations

I'm an experienced Agile practitioner based in Berlin, Germany, with a unique background spanning software development, team leadership, and enterprise agile coaching.

Currently working as Release Train Engineer at MBition GmbH, I facilitate Agile Release Trains for teams building the Android Automotive Operating System — one of the most complex software ecosystems in the automotive industry.

My journey started as a Software Team Leader and Java Trainer, giving me a deep technical foundation that makes me a uniquely effective Agile coach — I understand both the code and the culture.

I'm also a trained ICF-accredited coach (ACTP, Sola Unitas), which means my facilitation goes beyond frameworks into genuine human transformation.
